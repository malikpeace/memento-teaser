So, what is Memento?

Memento is something I've been building in the background for months, to help you beyond just videos.

Over the years, as I've continued to learn more about why or why not people achieve their goals, I've realized the common denominator between everyone who makes it is this equation: Focused Action x Consistency.

While this sounds really simple, most never do this. I've learned that most don't do this because they don't know what they want, what tangible actions to take, and how to keep going until they hit a positive feedback loop to make achievement automatic. So, I built this to help.

Memento is built to be the one place to make you actually follow through on what you want.

To help you get clear on what you truly desire and why, see the single highest-leverage move to make today, stay consistent until you get there, along with other systems to make sure you keep going and remember why you started.

Created to be a personal space, you evolve over time as you grow. A souvenir to keep you aligned on your dreams until you achieve them.

Why build this?

Well, over the years I've seen the same problems show up again and again across millions of people:

  • Lack of focus
  • Lack of direction
  • 10+ hour screen time
  • Being ambitious but lazy
  • Inability to be consistent for longer than 5 days
  • Knowing exactly what to do and still not doing it
  • Watching 1,000s of videos yet changing nothing

and on and on and on...

The modern world makes it easier and easier to fall into this trap, and most people never break out; they die living a mediocre life.

And you're reading this, I'm sure that's your biggest fear in the world, is leaving this world, knowing you didn't get the most out of your existence. You fear never becoming the person you know you can be, because you kept stopping yourself over and over and over again.

If we want to fix that, we need to change. We need a foundation to remind us to keep going when we inevitably run into resistance.

We need to find a real solution to this ever-growing problem of mediocrity.

Memento is that solution.

It's been exciting to learn and challenge myself in building this project. I can't wait for you to build your own memento :)
