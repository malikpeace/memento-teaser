So, what is Memento?

Memento is something I've been building in the background for months, to help you beyond just my videos. It's a system that helps you get aligned to what you truly want, finds the highest-leverage activities to move towards that goal, and becomes a personal, evolving tool that can be returned to and built on over time.

Over the years I've seen the same problems show up again and again in millions of people:

  • Lack of focus
  • Lack of direction
  • 10+ hour screen time
  • Being ambitious but lazy
  • Inability to be consistent for longer than 5 days
  • Knowing exactly what to do and still not doing it
  • Watching 1,000s of videos yet changing nothing
  • Not taking real, meaningful, high-leverage actions towards goals

and on and on and on...

Honestly, I'm fucking sick of it. I'm sure you are too.

Read hundreds of books, watch thousands of videos, study the people who actually made it, and you'll get a thousand different answers.

But the common denominator between all of them is that they took focused action, on one thing, over a long period of time.

That's how anything worthwhile gets done. (I honestly can't think of how you can achieve anything without that.)

If you just focus on that, it makes not achieving your goals unreasonable.

A lot of us know this, yet continue to do nothing.

Most overcomplicate it, have information overload, and don't have a space they can return to and get clear on their mission.

That's where Memento comes in.

Memento is built to be one place that helps you get clear on what you actually want, why you want it, find the highest-leverage action to get there, and stay consistent until you do.

The philosophy behind Memento is split into three main pillars:

Clarity
Get specific on what EXACTLY you want and WHY.
Action
Find the highest-leverage actions that ACTUALLY move you forward.
Consistency
Keep going until you love the progress, start a feedback loop, and create positive momentum.

I made Memento to help you fulfill all three, plus many other tools to keep you on track and aligned, all in one place, simplified, to significantly reduce failure.

Memento means remember in Latin, and mementos are objects that remind us of something specific.

This Memento is a personal souvenir, designed for you to build on and add to over time, to track your progress, remember why you started, remember the progress you've made, remember your time is limited, and remember to live true to you.

It's been exciting building this project BTS and I can't wait for you to build your memento. :)
